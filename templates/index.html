About FarmAI

Built for India's farmers, powered by AI

FarmAI is a final year project developed to help farmers across India identify crop diseases early and get actionable treatment advice in their own language — completely free.

The Problem

Crop diseases cause 20–40% yield loss globally. Indian farmers often lack access to agricultural experts, leading to delayed treatment and severe economic losses.

Our Solution

A deep learning model trained on 54,000 plant images that identifies 38 diseases in seconds. Results are provided in English, Hindi, and Odia with detailed treatment advice.

The Impact

By making disease detection accessible on any smartphone, FarmAI empowers farmers to act early, reduce losses, and choose between chemical and organic treatment options.
